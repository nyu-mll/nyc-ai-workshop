About

The NYC AI Workshop is a two-day introductory workshop on artificial intelligence and machine learning designed for early undergraduate students in New York City area. In particular, we welcome students without a computer science background. Coding experience is not required.

Participants will learn the basics of machine learning from leading experts, and hear about cutting-edge research applying AI to a variety of problems. They will also get first-hand experience in using machine learning algorithms, as well as learn about ways to pursue a career in AI.

The workshop is free to participate. We will cover all costs and provide food. The application will open soon! Sign up here to hear when the application goes live.

The NYC AI Workshop is organized by the members of NYU's Machine Learning for Lanugage Lab and generously funded by Moore-Sloan Data Science Environments.
