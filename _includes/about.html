About

The NYC AI Workshop is a two-day introductory workshop on artificial intelligence and machine learning, organized by the members of NYU's Machine Learning for Lanugage Lab. The workshop is designed to provide an introduction to AI and ML to early undergraduate students in New York City area. In particular, we welcome students without a computer science background. Coding experience is not required.

The workshop is free to participate. We will cover all costs and provide food. The application will open soon! Sign up here to get updates about when the application goes live.

NYC AI Workshop is generously funded by Moore-Sloan Data Science Environments.
