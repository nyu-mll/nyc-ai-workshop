About NYC AI Workshop

NYC AI Workshop is a 2-day introductory workshop on Artificial Intelligence, organized by the members of NYU's Machine Learning for Lanugage Lab.

Our motivation is to introduce AI to the undergraduate students in New York City area. The participants do not need to have Computer Science background or coding experience.

The workshop is free, and we will provide food and travel allowance to the participants.

The application will be open soon!

NYC AI Workshop is funded by Moore-Sloan Data Science Environments.
