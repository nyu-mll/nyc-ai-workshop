About

The NYC AI Workshop is a two-day introductory workshop on artificial intelligence and machine learning designed for early undergraduate students in New York City area. In particular, we welcome students without a computer science background. Coding experience is not required.

Participants will learn the fundamentals of machine learning from leading experts, and hear about cutting-edge research applying AI to a variety of problems. Participants will get first-hand experience in using machine learning techniques to build simple applications.

The workshop is free to participate. We will cover all costs and provide food. The application will open soon! Sign up here to get updates about when the application goes live.

The NYC AI Workshop is organized by the members of NYU's Machine Learning for Lanugage Lab and generously funded by Moore-Sloan Data Science Environments.
