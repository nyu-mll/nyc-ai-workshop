About NYC AI Workshop

The NYC AI Workshop is a 2-day introductory workshop on artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML), organized by the members of NYU's Machine Learning for Lanugage Lab.

Our mission is introduce AI and ML to early year undergraduate students in New York City area. In particular, this workshop is designed for people without a computer science background, so no coding experience is needed!

The workshop is application-based and free! And of course, there will be food.
